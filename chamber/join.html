Applicant Information
Membership Level:

Non-Profit

  • Recognition as a community-oriented organization supporting local initiatives.
  • Opportunities to collaborate with other non-profits for community projects.
  • Representation in local advocacy efforts related to non-profit issues.

Bronze

  • Inclusion in the Chamber's online business directory.
  • Discounts on tickets for Chamber-hosted business seminars and conferences.
  • Promotion of business events and promotions through the Chamber's social media channels.
  • Opportunity to feature in the Chamber's newsletter or blog.

Silver

  • Priority placement in the online business directory.
  • VIP access to exclusive networking events with key local business leaders.
  • Featured spot in the Chamber's newsletter and website for special promotions.
  • Sponsorship opportunities for Chamber events and programs.
  • Access to discounted business services such as legal consultations, marketing, and accounting.

Gold

  • Featured listing at the top of the online business directory.
  • Invitations to private, invitation-only events with high-profile community leaders and decision-makers.
  • Customized marketing campaigns targeting specific audience segments.
  • Opportunity to host Chamber-sponsored events and gain extensive brand exposure.
  • Dedicated Chamber representative for personalized assistance and support.
  • Priority access to business resources, government contacts, and funding opportunities.
