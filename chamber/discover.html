Views

Anawangin Cove

Discover paradise at Anawangin Cove in Botolan, Zambales. Nestled between pine trees and white sands, it's a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Take a boat ride from Pundaquit Beach and immerse yourself in the beauty of crystal-clear waters. Whether you're camping or trekking, find your perfect escape in the serenity of Anawangin Cove.

Bagsit River

Experience the untouched beauty of Bagsit River in Botolan, Zambales, where nature comes alive. This hidden gem offers a serene escape amidst lush greenery and pristine waters. Perfect for picnics, fishing, or simply unwinding, Bagsit River is a haven for nature enthusiasts. Immerse yourself in the tranquility of its surroundings and create unforgettable moments with your loved ones. Discover the magic of Bagsit River, where every visit promises an authentic connection with nature's wonders.

Camara Island

Embark on a journey to Camara Island, a tropical paradise nestled in the heart of Zambales, Philippines. With its powdery white sands, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, Camara Island offers a perfect escape for beach lovers and snorkeling enthusiasts. Accessible via a boat ride from Pundaquit Beach, the island beckons with its untouched beauty and tranquil ambiance. Dive into the mesmerizing underwater world, relax on the sun-kissed shores, and create memories that last a lifetime. Camara Island invites you to unwind, explore, and bask in the natural splendor of the Philippines.

Mount Pinatubo

Embark on the adventure of a lifetime through the Botolan Trail to Mount Pinatubo! Discover the raw beauty of nature as you trek through the scenic landscapes of Botolan, Zambales. This alternate route offers a unique and exhilarating journey to the majestic Mount Pinatubo, showcasing ancient lava flows, turquoise crater lakes, and surreal ash canyons. Guided by experienced locals, the Botolan Trail promises an unforgettable trekking experience. Conquer the iconic volcano, witness its breathtaking vistas, and create memories that will last a lifetime

Mount Tapulao

Rise to the challenge and conquer Mount Tapulao, the majestic peak standing tall in the heart of Zambales, Philippines. Known as the 'Poor Man's Pulag,' this trekking paradise offers adventurers a thrilling journey through lush jungles, cascading waterfalls, and panoramic vistas. As you ascend, feel the rush of adrenaline and the embrace of nature. Reaching the summit, you'll be rewarded with breathtaking views of rolling clouds and the surrounding landscapes. Mount Tapulao is not just a climb; it's a transformative experience, a test of your endurance, and an opportunity to connect with the raw beauty of the Philippines. So, gear up, take the challenge, and let the summit of Mount Tapulao be your crowning achievement..

San Felipe

Welcome to San Felipe, a charming coastal town in Zambales, Philippines, where the sun meets the sea and the heartwarming hospitality of locals awaits. With its pristine beaches, inviting azure waters, and a backdrop of rolling hills, San Felipe is a haven for beach lovers and nature enthusiasts alike. Dive into the clear waters, enjoy fresh seafood delicacies, and unwind in the embrace of nature. Whether you're seeking adventure or serenity, San Felipe offers a perfect blend of both. Come and discover the beauty of this hidden gem, where every sunrise brings a promise of a new adventure and every sunset paints the sky in hues of serenity.
